Memory Card Game

How to Play

  1. Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  2. The cards will be shuffled and placed face down on the board.
  3. Click on any card to reveal its symbol.
  4. Click on a second card to try and find its matching pair.
  5. If the symbols match, they remain face up.
  6. If they don't match, both cards will be flipped back over after a short delay.
  7. Match all pairs as quickly as you can and with the fewest moves possible.
  8. Your best times will appear in the scoreboard!
